Introduction

Welcome to Medtrack, the Health Monitoring System designed specifically for managing medical records and consultations for Persons Deprived of Liberty (PDL) at the San Jose District Jail, BJMP.

System Overview

Medtrack provides comprehensive tools for:

  • PDL Health Records Management - Track medical history, vital signs, and health conditions
  • Consultation Scheduling - Manage appointments and medical consultations
  • Medication Tracking - Monitor prescriptions and medication administration
  • Reporting & Analytics - Generate health reports and statistics
  • Calendar Integration - View and manage scheduled consultations
Tip: This system is optimized for use by medical professionals within the BJMP facility. All data is confidential and should be handled according to medical privacy regulations.

Getting Started

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
  • Internet connection
  • Valid user credentials provided by system administrator

Accessing the System

  1. Open your web browser
  2. Navigate to the Medtrack URL provided by your administrator
  3. You will be directed to the login page
Important: Never share your login credentials with anyone. Each user is responsible for activities performed under their account.

Login & Authentication

How to Login

1 Click on the "Login" button on the homepage

2 Enter your username in the Username field

3 Enter your password in the Password field

4 Click the "Login" button

After Successful Login

Upon successful authentication, you will see:

  • A welcome message with your username
  • "Go to Dashboard" button - Access the main doctor dashboard
  • "Logout" button - End your session securely

Logout

To logout safely:

  1. Click the red "Logout" button
  2. You will be redirected to the login page
  3. Your session will be terminated
Security Tip: Always logout when you finish using the system, especially on shared computers.

PDL Management

Viewing PDL List

Access the complete list of Persons Deprived of Liberty:

  1. Click "PDL List" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse through the list or use the search function
  3. Click on any PDL name to view their complete profile

PDL Profile Information

Each PDL profile contains:

Personal Information

  • Full name and photo
  • Date of birth and age
  • Gender and contact information
  • Emergency contact details

Detention Information

  • Current detention room
  • Admission date
  • Case details
  • Expected release date (if applicable)

Medical Information

  • Blood type
  • Known allergies
  • Chronic conditions
  • Current medications
  • Vaccination records

Vital Signs History

  • Blood pressure readings
  • Heart rate
  • Temperature
  • Weight and BMI
  • Respiratory rate

Adding a New PDL

1 Click "Add PDL" button

2 Fill in all required fields marked with *

3 Upload a photo (optional but recommended)

4 Enter medical history and allergies

5 Click "Save" to create the record

Editing PDL Information

  1. Navigate to the PDL's profile
  2. Click the "Edit" button
  3. Update the necessary information
  4. Click "Save Changes"
Important: Always verify PDL information before making changes. Incorrect medical information can lead to serious health risks.

Consultations

Scheduling a Consultation

1 From the dashboard, click "Add Consultation"

2 Select the PDL from the dropdown

3 Choose consultation date and time

4 Select consultation type:

  • Regular - Routine check-up
  • Follow-up - Post-treatment review
  • Emergency - Urgent medical attention
  • Dental - Dental care
  • Mental Health - Psychological consultation

5 Add notes or reason for consultation

6 Click "Schedule Consultation"

Conducting a Consultation

During the Consultation

  1. Open the consultation from your dashboard
  2. Record vital signs
  3. Document symptoms and complaints
  4. Enter diagnosis
  5. Prescribe medications if needed
  6. Add treatment notes
  7. Schedule follow-up if required

Recording Vital Signs

  • Blood Pressure (systolic/diastolic)
  • Heart Rate (bpm)
  • Temperature (°C or °F)
  • Weight (kg or lbs)
  • Height (cm)
  • Respiratory Rate
  • Oxygen Saturation (%)

Completing a Consultation

1 Review all entered information

2 Ensure all required fields are filled

3 Click "Mark as Completed"

4 The consultation will be saved to the PDL's medical history

Canceling a Consultation

  1. Open the scheduled consultation
  2. Click "Cancel Consultation"
  3. Provide a reason for cancellation
  4. Confirm cancellation
Best Practice: Always complete consultation records immediately after the visit while details are fresh. This ensures accurate medical documentation.

Medications

Viewing Medication List

Access the complete medication inventory:

  1. Click "Medications" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse available medications
  3. Use search to find specific drugs
  4. Filter by category or type

Prescribing Medication

1 During a consultation, click "Prescribe Medication"

2 Search and select the medication

3 Enter dosage information:

  • Dosage amount (e.g., 500mg)
  • Frequency (e.g., twice daily)
  • Duration (e.g., 7 days)
  • Route of administration (oral, injection, topical, etc.)

4 Add special instructions

5 Click "Add Prescription"

Medication Administration

Track when medications are given:

  1. View active prescriptions for a PDL
  2. Click "Record Administration"
  3. Enter date and time given
  4. Note any reactions or side effects
  5. Save the record

Managing Medication Inventory

Adding New Medication

  1. Click "Add Medication" button
  2. Enter medication details:
    • Generic name
    • Brand name
    • Category/Type
    • Strength/Dosage form
    • Stock quantity
    • Expiration date
  3. Click "Save"

Updating Stock Levels

  1. Find the medication in the list
  2. Click "Update Stock"
  3. Enter new quantity
  4. Add notes about the update
  5. Save changes
Safety Warning: Always check for drug interactions and allergies before prescribing. Verify dosages carefully to prevent medication errors.

Reports

Accessing Reports

  1. Click "Reports" in the navigation menu
  2. You'll see three main report categories:
    • Weekly Reports (Last 12 weeks)
    • Monthly Reports (Last 12 months)
    • Yearly Reports (Last 5 years)

Report Data

Each report shows:

  • Total - Total number of consultations
  • Completed - Successfully finished consultations
  • Scheduled - Upcoming appointments
  • Canceled - Canceled consultations
  • Emergencies - Emergency cases handled

Exporting Reports

Export data to CSV format:

  1. Click on the export option at the top of the page
  2. Choose the time period:
    • Weekly
    • Monthly
    • Yearly
  3. The CSV file will download automatically
  4. Open in Excel or other spreadsheet software

Using Report Data

Reports can be used for:

  • Tracking healthcare trends
  • Resource planning
  • Performance evaluation
  • Compliance documentation
  • Budget forecasting
Tip: Export reports regularly for backup and analysis purposes. Monthly exports are recommended for record-keeping.

Consultation Calendar

Viewing the Calendar

  1. Click "Consultation Calendar" in the navigation menu
  2. View consultations in different formats:
    • Month view - See the entire month
    • Week view - Focus on one week
    • Day view - Detailed daily schedule

Calendar Features

Color Coding

  • Blue - Regular consultations
  • Green - Completed consultations
  • Yellow - Scheduled/Upcoming
  • Red - Emergency cases
  • Gray - Canceled consultations

Quick Actions from Calendar

  • Click on any consultation to view details
  • Click on empty date/time to schedule new consultation
  • Drag and drop to reschedule (if enabled)
  • Right-click for quick menu options

Filtering Calendar View

  1. Use the filter options at the top
  2. Filter by:
    • Consultation type
    • Status
    • Specific PDL
    • Date range
  3. Click "Apply Filters"

Printing Calendar

  1. Select the desired view (day/week/month)
  2. Click the "Print" button
  3. Adjust print settings
  4. Print or save as PDF

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Cannot Login

  • Verify username and password are correct
  • Check Caps Lock is not enabled
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser
  • Contact system administrator if problem persists

Page Not Loading

  • Check internet connection
  • Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try accessing from a different device

Data Not Saving

  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check for error messages
  • Verify you have proper permissions
  • Try logging out and back in

Calendar Not Displaying

  • Refresh the page
  • Check date range filters
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  • Try a different browser

Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered here:

  1. Document the error message or issue
  2. Note what you were trying to do
  3. Take a screenshot if possible
  4. Contact your system administrator
  5. Provide all relevant details
