ABOUT THE FACILITY

San Jose District Jail
BJMP, Occidental Mindoro

The Bureau of Jail Management and Penology (BJMP) San Jose District Jail serves as the primary detention facility in San Jose, Occidental Mindoro. MedTrack supports the facility's mandate to uphold the health rights of every PDL.

Designed with medical staff and administrators in mind, this system streamlines documentation and care delivery within a secure, role-based environment.
